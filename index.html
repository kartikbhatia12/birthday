EPISODE 1

Romantic Comedy (The Rumoured Couple) ❤️

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 2019 • School • Comedy

One innocent boy, One kamini ladki. One classroom. One hundred rumours. Somehow everyone believed Me and You were secretly dating... except Me and You.

Story

It all started on 12th April 2019. We talked for the first time in that stupid Truth & Dare game with people we don't even want to remember. 😜 And then it all started. We barely knew what was happening... but somehow our classmates had already written a complete love story. Specially Ankit, Raj, Deep, Jay(Tera uss time ka crush) 👀, Ishu, etc. etc. Every day came with new rumours, new teasing, and new awkward moments. Looking back... it was actually hilarious 😂.
Honestly, uss time mujhe bhi lagta tha ki hnn shyd kuch toh hai hum dono ke beech mei kyunki remember jis tarah se ek baar tu mera number lene ke liye piche padh gyi thi yahan tak ki meri diary se number dekhne ki koshish karti thi 😂.
But ab sochta hu ki acha hai tera saath waisa kuch nahi hua mera bach gya mai 😎😁..

EPISODE 2

GHOST PROTOCOL 👻

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 2020 • Friendship • Comedy

Every friendship has that one person who disappears after reading messages. This episode uncovers the mysterious ghosting incidents that almost became a national emergency.

Story

It all started when messages suddenly stopped getting replies. Every conversation followed the same pattern.
"Hello."
Seen.
Silence.
One hour passed.
Then two.
Then three.
Then another.
Then an entire day.
The Friendship Investigation Bureau immediately launched Operation Ghost Protocol. Scientists, detectives and highly trained experts were unable to determine whether the subject was busy...
asleep...
or simply ignoring Kartik on purpose. 👀
Surprisingly, after disappearing for hours (sometimes days 😂), she would casually return as if absolutely nothing had happened.
To this day, the mystery remains officially unsolved.
I know tera ye sab padh ke kya reaction hoga
"Haawwwwww..... Kutta kahin ka, maine kab kara ye sab tujhe mai maar daalungi haramzade"
But tu khud bata tere birthday pe apni galti accept kyu karu 😁
Tera birthday Teri galti 😎😉

EPISODE 3

THE GREAT FIGHT 💥

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 2021 • Drama • Friendship

Every friendship has one season where a completely unnecessary third character enters the story and somehow creates chaos.

Story

Every great friendship has one season where the writers decide,

"Let's introduce unnecessary drama." 🎬

And in our story... that role was perfectly played by...
someone's boyfriend. 😂

One day everything was completely normal. We used to talk, annoy each other, roast each other, and somehow survive another school day.

Then suddenly... everything changed.
Messages became shorter.
Conversations became awkward.
And before I could even understand what was happening... I somehow became "The Problem." 😭😂

The Friendship Investigation Bureau immediately launched Operation: Save The Friendship.

After months of investigation... hundreds of witness statements... and absolutely zero useful evidence... the Bureau finally discovered the real cause.

Third Party Interference. 👀

Translation?
Tera uss time ka boyfriend. 😂

Because of him... our friendship slowly started disappearing.
No huge fight.
No dramatic shouting.
Just awkwardness... distance... and silence.

Honestly,

Looking back now... it's honestly funny.

Imagine... we survived classroom rumours, school gossip, teachers, assignments, and even the legendary "Tum dono pakka relationship mein ho" conspiracy. 😂😂

Aur phir friendship almost khatam ho gayi because of one new character's entry. 🎭

Waise... agar uss time tu thodi kam bewakoof hoti... toh shayad Episode 3 kabhi banta hi nahi. 😌😂

But don't worry... every good Netflix series eventually gets renewed. ❤️

EPISODE 4

THE NEVER ENDING COMEBACK ♻️❤️

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 2021-2026 • Friendship • Drama • Comedy

Every time they stopped talking... destiny somehow managed to reconnect them. Or maybe... every breakup came with one free Kartik. 😂

Story

Episode 3 ke baad... officially humari baat almost band ho gayi thi. 😶

Maine socha... "Bas bhai... iss series ka THE END ho gaya." 🎬

Lekin... kuch time baad... ek din randomly phone aata hai. 📞

Madam ka breakup ho gaya tha school wale boyfriend se. 😂

Thodi der baat hui... phir daily baat hone lagi... aur mujhe laga... "Accha... friendship reboot ho gayi." ❤️

Lekin... sirf 1-2 mahine hi hue the... tabhi ek din madam ne bade casually bola...

"Waise mera naya boyfriend ban gaya coaching mein." 😌

Maine bola... "Very nice." 👍

Aur phir... messages kam... calls kam... replies kam... aur finally...

"Usne mana kiya hai tumse baat karne." 🤡

Friendship Investigation Bureau ne pehla conclusion nikala...

Boyfriend Detected = Kartik Rejected. 😂

Phir kuch mahine complete silence. 📵

Aur ek din... phir se phone. 📞

Is baar... boyfriend se ladai ho gayi thi. 😭

"Yaar baat kar na..." 😭

Phir kuch hafton tak baat... phir sab normal... aur honestly... iss baar main bhi kabhi kabhi Ghost Mode activate kar deta tha. 👻😂

Kuch aur mahine baad... phir ek aur phone. 😂

"Mera breakup ho gaya." 💀

Aur phir... Friendship Season 3 restart. ❤️

Kuch mahine sab mast chala... phir iss baar college mein ek naya ladka pasand aa gaya. 😂

Maine andar hi andar socha...

"Lo ji... naya season shuru." 🎬

Aur exactly wahi hua. 😭

Naya boyfriend.
Baat kam.
Phir... "Usne mana kiya hai." 🤦‍♂️

Matlab... script copy-paste chal rahi thi. 😂

Har baar same cycle.

Boyfriend.
Baat band.
Breakup ya ladai.
Phone Kartik ko.
Kuch mahine friendship.
Repeat. 🔄😂

Mere dost toh mujhe literally bolte hain...

"Bhai... ye tujhe tissue paper ki tarah use karti hai." 🤣🤣

Aur sach bolu... kabhi kabhi mujhe bhi lagta tha... "Shayad ye log sahi hi bol rahe hain." 😂

Lekin... plot twist abhi baaki tha. 😌

Cut to... February 2026. 📅

Subah... 8:30 baje. 😴

Main mast so raha tha... tabhi phone bajta hai. 📞

Aadhe hosh mein phone uthaya... toh saamne se awaaz aayi...

"Yaar... I fucked up... mujhse bahut badi galti ho gayi." 😭

Main poori neend mein tha... toh maine seedha pooch diya...

"Pregnant ho gayi kya?" 💀🤣

Do second complete silence. 😂😂😂

Phir pata chala... Madam ka... once again... breakup ho gaya tha. 🤦‍♂️

Aur isi wajah se mujhe phone kiya gaya. 😂

Lekin... iss baar kuch alag hua. ❤️

Is baar baat phir se shuru hui... aur surprisingly... ab tak chal bhi rahi hai. 😌

Shayad hum dono finally mature ho gaye... Ya phir... tujhe finally koi boyfriend nahi mila jo mana kare. 🤣🤣

Friendship Investigation Bureau ka official conclusion...

"This friendship has survived more breakups than most relationships." 😂❤️
